About Us

OMMACHEM is a comprehensive supplier of textile auxiliaries, providing a full range of solutions from pre-treatment, dyeing bath, cleaning, soaping, stripping, color fixing to finishing agents. Our products are developed with a strong commitment to eco-friendliness, energy efficiency, and sustainability.

We are dedicated to helping our clients solve problems in every stage of textile processing. With professional technical support and reliable product quality, OMMACHEM delivers tailored solutions to meet the evolving needs of global textile manufacturers.
